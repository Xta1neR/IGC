About International Global Conference

International Global Conference :

IGC-MUN is more than just a conference, It is a convergence of global intellect and empathy. Our vision is to create an inclusive and impactful platform where delegates from diverse backgrounds can unite to deliberate, negotiate, and innovate solutions to the world's most pressing challenges.

 

1ST International Global Conference - Model United Nations, an extraordinary event

hosted by Lovely Professional University that is set to make history. For the first time, we are extending our heartfelt invitation to delegates from across the globe

and Pan India, inviting them to gather at our prestigious institution for a monumental discourse on global issues and a collective mission to raise global awareness.

In this transformative event passionate young leaders from around the world will unite to address global challenges, enhance their skills, and broaden their perspectives. Together, they will drive positive change and illuminate a brighter path for generations to come.

Objectives of Conference

Ø Global Diversity: IGC-MUN boasts a global delegation representing diverse cultures and nations, fostering a dynamic exchange of ideas. We firmly believe that diverse perspectives lead to comprehensive solutions.

 

Ø Knowledge Exchange: With thought-provoking committees and themes, IGC-MUN encourages the sharing of knowledge and in-depth exploration of complex issues. Delegates will emerge with a profound understanding of the challenges at hand.

 

Ø Leadership Development: We champion the potential of young leaders. IGC-MUN provides a unique opportunity for personal and professional growth, nurturing leadership skills, enhancing public speaking abilities, and refining diplomatic expertise

Guidelines

-       Eligibility

-       International Global Conference (IGC-2023) Model United Nations welcomes youth between the ages of 15 to 25 years old from any country who wish to have experience in model united nations conferences or have a high interest in global affairs to be the delegate of IGC-2023 Model United Nations.

-        

-       Program Mechanism

-       1.     The International Global Conference will be using the offline concept of Model United Nations.

-       2.     The conference will consist of International and National delegates.

-       3.     All matters regarding the country and council allocation will be arranged based on the decision of the committee.

-       4.     All conference participants must follow COVID-19 safety and health protocol that has been regulated by the authorities of India.

-       Registration Mechanism

-       1.     The registrations will be accepted on the first come and first serve basis.

-       2.     Registration is open for various categories, which includes non-accommodation, accommodation, meal and travel assistance in separate categories.

-       3.     The delegates can avail the group booking facilities under the group size of : 2 members, 3 to 5 members and 6 to 9 members.

-       4.     The conference provides a special category for school delegation which is available for the schools that provide a team of minimum 15 student participants.
